About

BananaCode.org is a non-profit organizational website. This website is made to help local freelance web developers, designers, UI/UX developers, product developers, etc. in the Philippines to show their talents and outputs to the world. Thanks to its founder, Peter John Reyes, programmers in the Philippines can let their talents and hardworks be known internationally.
